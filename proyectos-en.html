A software made with open source artificial intelligence libraries, with the purpose of giving information to visitors of the university.

First of all, it detects if a human is in front of the camera (face recognition), and then it asks if it can help (text-to-speach), by providing options like:

  • 1.- Where are the nearest restroom?
  • 2.- Where can I find the administrative building?
  • 3.- How to register for the next semester?
  • 4.- Cancel.

Depending on which number the person chooses (voice recognition), the assistant will give the corresponding information.
